Your Privacy
NOTICE OF PRIVACY PRACTICES

How We Protect Your Health Information

Walden Bailey Chiropractic is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your health information in accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).

We collect only the minimum necessary health information to provide you with quality chiropractic care. Your information is never sold, shared with third parties for marketing purposes, or used in any way that is not directly related to your care or as required by law.

You have the right to access your health records, request corrections, and receive an accounting of disclosures. To exercise these rights or for questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at (716) 893-9200 or visit our office at 1086 Walden Ave, Suite 1, Buffalo, NY 14211.

This website does not collect any protected health information (PHI). All appointment scheduling and clinical communication occurs by phone or in person.
